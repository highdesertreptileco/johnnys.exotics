Who we are

We’re a Sparks, Nevada–based reptile room built around calm handling, clean environments, and consistent routines. Johnny’s Exotics began as a kid’s passion for reptiles and has grown into a carefully maintained space where animal welfare and education come first. Every reptile in our care is supported with proper habitats, enrichment, and daily observation. We focus on understanding behavior, respecting boundaries, and creating low-stress environments that allow animals to thrive. Our goal is simple: to show reptiles as they truly are—fascinating, intelligent, and deserving of thoughtful care. Through shared moments and close-up views, we aim to help others learn, appreciate, and see these animals in a new light.

Education-first. Animal welfare always.
